Tiramisu

Description

This exquisite italian dessert is usually reserved for special events. Luckily, this dish is easy to make at home. Just gather the ingredients and follow the instructions for a great italian dessert at home.

Ingredients

  • 40 to 45 ladyfinger cookies (depends on the bowl)
  • 500 grams of mascarpone cheese
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Coffee
  • Vanilla essence
  • Amaretto or another spirit
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Open the eggs and separated the whites from the yolks. Cook the yolks on a double boiler or bain-marie with half a cup of sugar, integrating it. In another bowl, whisk the whites until firm, but not merengue consistency.
  2. Once you have your whisked whites and the other preparation is not hot, pour the yolks-sugar syrup onto the whites, slowly. To integrate better and not lose air, fold gently the mix.
  3. Add the mascarpone cheese slowly, folding slowly. Add a touch of vanilla essence.
  4. Prepare three strong cups of coffee, and add a liquor. Personally I use amaretto, 1/8 of a cup, to 3 cups of cofffee.
  5. Dip the ladyfingers completely in the coffee, but remove quickly, three seconds tops. You want to fully coat the cookies, but not make them soggy.
  6. In a bowl, leave the ladyfingers side by side, layering the bowl. You should be able to build two layers. Once you complete the first layer, cover it with the mix previously made. Do this again with a second layer of cookies, and a second layer of the mix.
  7. Sift cocoa powder over the second layer of mix, and put it in the refrigerator for a couple of hours.
  8. Enjoy!
